SpongeBob SquarePants
1999 ‧ Comedy ‧ 15 seasons
SpongeBob SquarePants is an iconic animated series created by Stephen Hillenburg, following the antics of SpongeBob, an optimistic and goofy sea sponge who works at the Krusty Krab, a fast-food restaurant under the sea. With his quirky friends, including the grumpy Squidward and his best friend Patrick, SpongeBob navigates life in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom. The show is known for its slapstick humor, surreal world-building, and underlying philosophical themes.
