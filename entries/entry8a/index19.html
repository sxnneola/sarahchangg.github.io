Sofia the First
2012 ‧ Adventure ‧ 4 seasons
Sofia the First is an animated series about a young girl who becomes a princess after her mother marries King Roland II. The show follows Sofia's journey as she learns what it means to be a princess, with the help of magical creatures, royal friends, and her talking animal friends. Sofia the First emphasizes themes of kindness, courage, and self-discovery, appealing to young audiences with its colorful animation and heartwarming stories.
